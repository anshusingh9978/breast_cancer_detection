Breast Cancer Detection

Breast cancer detection saves lives by finding cancer early, often before symptoms appear. Early detection makes treatment easier and more effective, increasing the chances of full recovery. It also means less aggressive treatments, lower medical costs, and a better quality of life for patients. Overall, detecting breast cancer in its early stages is crucial for improving health outcomes and giving patients the best chance to overcome the disease.

The model aims to assist healthcare professionals in early diagnosis and treatment planning.
